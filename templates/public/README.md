# KOL Connect

KOL Connect is a platform that connects **Companies (Brands)** with **Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs)** such as influencers and content creators.  
The platform allows companies to create marketing campaigns while KOLs can browse campaigns and collaborate with brands.

The system includes **two dashboards**:

- Company Dashboard
- KOL (Influencer) Dashboard

This helps both sides manage influencer marketing campaigns efficiently.

---

# Project Overview

Influencer marketing is one of the fastest growing digital marketing strategies.  
However, brands often struggle to find the right influencers, and influencers struggle to find good collaborations.

**KOL Connect solves this by providing:**

- A platform for companies to create campaigns
- A system for influencers to find collaborations
- Dashboards to manage campaigns and profiles
- Campaign tracking and management tools

---

# User Roles

## 1. Company (Brand)

Companies use the platform to:

- Create marketing campaigns
- Discover influencers
- Manage campaign budgets
- Track collaborations

---

## 2. KOL (Influencer)

KOLs use the platform to:

- Create influencer profiles
- Browse available campaigns
- Apply for collaborations
- Track earnings

---

# Company Dashboard Features

After logging in, companies can access the **Company Dashboard**.

### Dashboard
Provides an overview of:

- Active campaigns
- Campaign statistics
- Quick access to major features

---

### Company Profile
Manage company information such as:

- Company Name
- Email
- Industry
- Profile updates

---

### Create Campaigns
Companies can create campaigns by defining:

- Campaign title
- Campaign description
- Budget
- Requirements

---

### Browse KOLs
Companies can browse available influencers and view:

- Influencer profiles
- Social media platforms
- Content niches
- Audience details

---

### Campaigns
Manage campaigns including:

- Active campaigns
- Pending campaigns
- Completed campaigns

---

### Wallet
Manage financial transactions including:

- Campaign budgets
- Wallet balance
- Payments to influencers

---

### Settings
Allows companies to:

- Update company details
- Change account password

---

# KOL Dashboard Features

KOLs (Influencers) have their own dashboard.

### Dashboard
Shows:

- Available campaigns
- Active collaborations
- Earnings summary

---

### KOL Profile
Influencers can manage their professional profile:

- Name
- Bio
- Social media links
- Content niche
- Audience information

---

### Browse Campaigns
Influencers can explore campaigns created by companies.

Campaign details include:

- Brand name
- Campaign description
- Requirements
- Payment details

---

### My Campaigns
Shows campaigns that the influencer has:

- Applied to
- Been selected for
- Completed

---

### Earnings / Wallet
Track earnings from campaigns including:

- Total earnings
- Pending payments
- Completed payments

---

### Settings
Allows influencers to:

- Update profile details
- Change password

---

# How the Platform Works

1. Companies create marketing campaigns.
2. KOLs browse campaigns available on the platform.
3. Influencers apply for campaigns.
4. Companies review and select influencers.
5. Campaign collaboration begins.
